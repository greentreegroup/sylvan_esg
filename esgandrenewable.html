ESG And Renewable

Renewables

Investment in renewable energy is an important step to address global climate change. Increasing the supply of renewable energy directly replaces carbon-intensive energy sources and significantly reduces greenhouse gas emissions. In recent years, the renewable energy industry has undergone significant change. The advancement of renewable technologies and rapid reductions in cost have accelerated the deployment of renewable solutions.
Sylvan covers the complete suite of clean energy technologies ranging from wind, solar, and hydro to emerging technologies, such as batteries and storage solutions. Our professionals have extensive, practical experience in applying knowledge to drive decisions and create impact.

  • Solar

    Why Invest in Solar?

    Solar energy, primarily through solar photovoltaics, is one of the leading sources of renewable energy generation globally. With highly competitive levelized costs, innovative technology and business models and the ability to provide flexible and scalable renewable energy both behind the meter and at the utility level, the sector is poised for continuous growth.

    Solar Advisory Service

    Sylvan has a long history of helping companies create lasting impact in their communities. Our work with solar developers provides an opportunity to build additional impact – improving community access to clean energy and invigorating local economies.
    We provide financing to solar developers who are creating high-impact solar projects across the U.S.  We also apply our tax-advantaged capital management experience to build diversified investment portfolios across the solar industry.  
    Our institutional quality investment strategy is underpinned by the best practices of an SEC-registered investment advisor. With decades of experience in the clean power generation and tax credit investment sectors, our solar investment professionals have deep industry relationships.  A well-defined process for sourcing, selecting, analyzing, executing, and asset management ensures that capital is deployed efficiently and cost-effectively, and in compliance with our investment strategy.
